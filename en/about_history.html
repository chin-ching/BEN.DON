A history of Chin Ching：

  • 1977
    Established Chin Ching Silica Sand Co., Ltd., built Tunglo Plant for producing silica sand and resin coated sand.
  • 1981
    Established Chin Ching Mines Co., Ltd., to survey and mine the high purity material for silica sand.
  • 1983
    Built a silica powder plant in Touwu.
  • 1985
    Started to produce phenolic resin. Besides for our resin coated sand usage, also exporting it to overseas clients.
  • 1988
    Acquired Kuang Ying Mines Co., Ltd. for producing foundry sand and glass sand.
  • 1990
    Established K. L. Chemicals Co., Ltd.
  • 1992
    Be the agent of Vietnam Camranh Bay silica sand, and built an imported silica sand and production line in Chin Ching Touwu Plant for supplying high purity silica sand and powder.
  • 1995
    Built a reclaiming sand production line in Chin Ching Tunglo Plant
  • 1997
    K. L. Chemicals Co., Ltd. was certified by ISO-9002:1994.
  • 1998
    K. L. Chemicals Co., Ltd. successfully developed the phenolic resin for firebrick used in refractory industry. Also cooperated with Industrial Technology Research Institute, together developed the PF forming resin.
  • 1999
    Continuously exchanging technologies with Dongguan, Suzhou, Tianjin and Thailand resin coated sand manufacturers.
  • 2001
    Chin Ching Silica Sand Co., Ltd. was certified by ISO-9001:2000.
  • 2002
    K.L Chemicals Co., Ltd. and Chin Ching Silica Sand Co., Ltd. successfully developed the hardener and filler for EMC used in IC encapsulation and silica powder for IC encapsulation use.
  • 2003
    K. L. Chemicals Co., Ltd. was certified by ISO-9001:2000. and successfully developed the phenolic resin for painting.
  • Chin Ching Touwu Plant was expanded with factory area, office, and QC unit and thus had independent self-inspection ability.
  • 2004
    K. L. Chemicals Co., Ltd. successfully developed Low Hexamine phenolic resin.
  • Chin Ching Touwu Plant set up indirect drying equipments. Tunglo Plant successfully developed RCS with low expansibility, heat resistant and high tensile strength.
  • 2006
    Chin Ching developed silica powder for Cold cathode fluorescent lamp (CCFL).
  • 2008
    K.L. added the production line of hardeners for EMC and CCL substrates for overseas customers.
  • 2012
    Chin Ching Zhongxing Plant officially started operation, producing silica sand and resin coated sand.
  • 2014
    K.L. improved production process and wastewater treatment technology, dramatically reduce emissions of waste water and optimized waste water’s Chemical Oxygen Demand (COD).
  • Chin Ching Touwu plant developed silica sand and silica powder for TFT-LCD panels.
  • 2016
    Chin Ching and K.L. cooperated with the government’s guidance on the 3D printing sand mold technology project, and successfully developed the sand materials and bonding materials for 3D printing sand molds.
  • 2018
    Chin Ching Tongluo plant renovation was completed, producing silica sand and silica Powder for photovoltaic use.
  • The headquarter was relocated to the current site of Songjiang Road, Taipei.
  • 2019
    Development of environmentally friendly low ammonia resin coated sand.
  • All plants’ production fuel was fully converted to natural gas to make a contribution to environmental protection.
  • Kuanying added artificial sand recycling production line.
  • Agent for German HUETTENES-ALBERTUS company for casting materials.
  • Agent for Japan AGCC inorganic materials for 3D printing precision casting.
